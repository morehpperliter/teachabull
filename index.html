Capital allocation through Teachabull is subject to TTS score, plan tier, and review cycle outcomes. Allocations may be reduced or withdrawn at any time based on performance. No specific outcome is guaranteed.

The Trader Track Score (TTS) and Risk Governance Operating System (RGOS) are proprietary frameworks. They assess behavioral patterns and do not guarantee profitability. Traders are solely responsible for their own decisions. Teachabull does not provide investment advice.
